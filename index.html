Privacy Policy for Middlesex App

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to the Middlesex app ("The unofficial school app for Middlesex"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application. The App is designed to provide Middlesex School students with access to schedules, menus, announcements, and sports information.

Important Notice: This app is NOT an official Middlesex School application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by Middlesex School. This is an independent student-created app developed by Nicholas Noon. While the app provides access to school-related information and content, it is maintained independently and not by the school administration.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Name and Grade

  • You provide your name and grade level when setting up your profile
  • This information is used to personalize your experience and deliver relevant announcements

Schedule Information

  • You can input your class schedule manually or by taking a photo of your schedule
  • Schedule photos are processed using OpenAI's Vision API to automatically extract class information
  • Photos are transmitted securely and are not permanently stored

Custom Class Requests

  • You may request custom classes to be added to your schedule
  • These requests are reviewed by school administrators

Device Information

  • We collect device push notification tokens to deliver announcements and sports updates
  • This allows us to send you relevant notifications about school events and activities

Automatically Collected Information

User ID

  • When you use the App, a unique identifier is created through Apple's CloudKit service
  • This identifier is used to sync your data across your devices

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

App Functionality

  • Authenticate your access to the App
  • Display your personalized class schedule
  • Sync your preferences across devices
  • Deliver push notifications for announcements and sports updates
  • Enable Live Activities for real-time sports score updates
  • Process schedule photos to extract class information

Service Improvement

  • Maintain and improve App performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

Apple CloudKit

  • All user data is stored using Apple's CloudKit service
  • Data is encrypted and secured according to Apple's privacy standards
  • CloudKit is covered under Apple's privacy policies

OpenAI Vision API

  • When you take a photo of your schedule, the image is sent to OpenAI for processing
  • OpenAI's API extracts class information from the photo
  • Images are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored by us or OpenAI
  • OpenAI's data handling is governed by their terms of service

Apple WeatherKit

  • Weather data for the Middlesex School campus is provided by Apple's WeatherKit
  • No personal location data is collected; weather is based on the school's fixed location

Apple Push Notification Service (APNs)

  • Push notifications are delivered through Apple's notification service
  • Device tokens are managed securely and used only for notification delivery

Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data Is Stored

  • All user data is stored in Apple's CloudKit infrastructure
  • Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Your data is associated with your iCloud account

Data Security

  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • Access to administrative functions is restricted to authorized school personnel
  • API keys and sensitive credentials are stored securely using iOS Keychain

Data Sharing

We Do Not:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Share your data with data brokers
  • Track you across other apps or websites
  • Display advertisements in the App

We May Share Data:

  • With school administrators for the purposes of managing custom class requests and announcements
  • When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • You can view and update your name, grade, and schedule information within the App
  • You can control notification preferences in the App settings

Data Deletion

  • You can delete your account data by removing the App and clearing your iCloud data
  • Contact us at middlesex@nicholasnoon.com to request data deletion assistance

Opt-Out

  • You can disable push notifications in your device settings
  • You can choose not to use the schedule photo import feature

Age Requirements

This app is intended exclusively for high school students at Middlesex School, who are typically 14 years of age or older. The app is not designed for or directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13 years of age.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you have an active account and use the App. When you delete the App or request data deletion, your personal information will be removed from our systems, subject to any legal retention requirements.

School-Generated Content

Announcements, Menus, and Schedules

  • Content such as announcements, dining hall menus, and special schedules are created and managed by authorized Middlesex School administrators
  • This content is provided for informational purposes and is not considered personal data

Sports Information

  • Sports schedules, scores, and updates are managed by authorized school personnel
  • Live score updates are provided for school athletic events

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing an in-app notification if the changes are significant

Your continued use of the App after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

International Users

The App is designed for students at Middlesex School located in Concord, Massachusetts, USA. Data is stored on servers located in the United States through Apple's CloudKit service.
