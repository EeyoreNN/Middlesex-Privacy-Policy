Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up my schedule?

You can either manually enter your classes or use the camera feature to scan your printed schedule. The AI will automatically extract your class information.

Why am I not receiving announcements?

Make sure you have:

  • Enabled notifications in iOS Settings > Notifications > Middlesex
  • Selected your grade level in the app settings
  • An active internet connection

How do I update my profile information?

Go to the Settings tab in the app to update your name, grade, and notification preferences.

The menu isn't showing today's meals

Pull down on the Dining tab to refresh. Menu data is updated by app administrators and may occasionally be delayed.

How do I request a custom class?

If your class isn't in the system, you can submit a custom class request through the Schedule tab. It will be reviewed by app administrators.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete your account data by:

  • Removing the app from your device
  • Clearing your iCloud data associated with the app
  • Contacting support for manual deletion: nnoon@mxschool.edu

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is stored using Apple's CloudKit service with encryption. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

The app is crashing or not working properly

Try these steps:

  1. Force quit the app and reopen it
  2. Check for app updates in the App Store
  3. Restart your device
  4. If the problem persists, email nnoon@mxschool.edu with details about the issue

Report a Bug

Found a bug? Please email nnoon@mxschool.edu with:

  • Description of the problem
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • Screenshots if applicable
